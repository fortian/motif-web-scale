What is this?

The Motif Mentor Smart Scale is a neat Bluetooth based scale, that has crappy app support. This web app adds what everyone asks for when they get the scale: "How do I just weight something?!?" The scale is a Bluetooth Low Energy device ,and Google Chrome has Web Bluetooth support, so I wrote this web app to connect to the Motif Perfect scale and just do one thing well: weigh things.
